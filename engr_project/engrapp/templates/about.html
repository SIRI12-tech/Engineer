Our History

Founded in 1995, Electrical Engineering Co. has been at the forefront of electrical innovation for over two decades. From our humble beginnings as a small local contractor, we've grown into a leading provider of comprehensive electrical engineering solutions, serving clients across the nation.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, sustainable, and reliable electrical solutions that empower businesses and communities to thrive in an increasingly electrified world.

Our Vision

To be the leading force in shaping a future where clean, efficient, and intelligent electrical systems enhance every aspect of life and industry.
